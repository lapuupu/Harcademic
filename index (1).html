Advice from tutors

Study smarter, not harder.

🧠
Spaced repetition
Review material at increasing intervals rather than cramming. Apps like Anki make this automatic.
✍️
Teach it back
Explain a concept out loud as if teaching someone else. If you can't, you haven't learned it yet.
📵
Single-tasking
One subject per session. Phone in another room. 45 focused minutes beats 3 distracted hours.
❓
Ask early
Don't wait until the night before a test. Confusion compounds — address it in the first week.

What we stand for

Our values

Accessibility first
Academic help should never depend on what your family can afford. Every session we offer is completely free, no strings attached.
Peer understanding
Students who recently learned something often explain it better than teachers. We leverage that — our tutors get it because they've been there.
No judgment
Asking for help is brave. We create a space where no question is too basic, and no student feels embarrassed for not knowing something.
Community ownership
This club belongs to the students. Our tutors volunteer their time because they believe in the mission — and you can too.
